What documents do I need to apply for a Gulf job from Hyderabad?

You need a valid passport (minimum 2-year validity), educational/skill certificates, experience letters, passport-size photographs, and a medical fitness certificate. For healthcare roles, additional professional licenses may be required.

How long does the Gulf visa process typically take from India?

The complete Gulf visa process typically takes 30-45 days from India: document attestation (7-10 days), employer visa application (10-15 days), medical + GAMCA clearance (3-5 days), and visa stamping + emigration (5-7 days).
